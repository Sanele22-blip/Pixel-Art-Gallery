Mental health

Mental health is vital because it affects all parts of our lives. Just like physical health, it impacts our happiness and how well we cope with life. By taking care of our mental health, we can handle stress better and lead more fulfilling lives. It's important to talk about mental health openly and make sure everyone can get the support they need to thrive.

Here are three international hotlines you can contact when you need mental assistance:
1.International Suicide Hotlines: These helplines offer support for individuals experiencing suicidal thoughts or feelings. They often provide confidential counseling and crisis intervention services.
2.International Crisis Hotlines: These helplines offer immediate support and assistance for individuals in crisis, including those experiencing mental health crises or emotional distress.
3.International Mental Health Support Lines: Some organizations provide toll-free numbers or helplines specifically dedicated to providing mental health support and information to individuals worldwide.
